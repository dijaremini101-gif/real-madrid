About Real Madrid

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, commonly referred to as Real Madrid, is a professional football club based in Madrid, Spain.

Founded on 6 March 1902 as Madrid Football Club, the club has traditionally worn a white home kit since inception.

The word Real is Spanish for "Royal" and was bestowed to the club by King Alfonso XIII in 1920 along with the royal crown in the emblem.

Real Madrid is the most successful club in European football history, holding a record 15 UEFA Champions League titles and 36 La Liga championships.

What is REAL MADRID focused about in the next 3 years

Real Madrid is focusing on three big things for the next few years:

Young talent. The club is signing and developing young stars like Jude Bellingham, Vinícius Jr., Rodrygo, and Endrick. These players will lead the team for the next decade.

Data and technology. Real Madrid is using advanced data analytics and AI to improve player performance, prevent injuries, and make smarter tactical decisions.

Stadium experience. The renovated Santiago Bernabéu is becoming a year-round entertainment hub with a retractable roof, 360° video screen, and underground greenhouse for the pitch.

Past success: 15 Champions League titles (most ever), 36 La Liga titles, 5 FIFA Club World Cups. Recent success: 2024 Champions League winners, 2024 La Liga champions, back-to-back European finals dominance.
